why to join Innocamp?

Innocamp helps the young Indian generation who had benefited nothing from our country’s poor education system by providing an intensive industry relevant training on the leading software technologies.

Innocamp program is completely FREE upfront and our students pay zero tuition fee until they land a job. We believe everyone should get the right opportunities to learn, hence we do not charge any fees upfront.

Innocamp is not just another training institute where you go learn something and leave forever. Innocamp is a community of high performance people who support each other and to win together. Each Innoocamp grad will always be in touch with our community and help the new buddies as a way of giving back
